Data migration, simplified.

Making your business more profitable shouldn't be rocket science.

But when you’re paying huge upfront costs to run on-premise servers, you’re spending too much money on data – and the prospect of moving all that data to the cloud might seem overwhelming.


So what’s a better alternative? Automating the data migration process means your company will save time, money, and resources. You’ll be able to skip the risks and reap the benefits of moving to the cloud, and put your business on the path to becoming more profitable.


BitTitan’s MigrationWiz automates the entire data migration process. Through an intuitive cloud-based interface, it allows users to conduct seamless, secure migrations of mailboxes, public folders, PST files, documents, archives and data – completely on-demand, and from anywhere.
